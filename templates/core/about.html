About Me

Professional Journey

With a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from Pak-Austria Fachhochschule (2020–2024) and a Dean's List distinction in Summer 2023, I laid a strong foundation in web technologies and user-centered design during internships at Robotic World (HTML/CSS/Bootstrap) and Matrix Tech (WordPress, Figma) in 2021–2022.

In Summer 2023 I advanced into full-stack development at Giga Developers—designing database schemas with Django ORM, building RESTful APIs, and collaborating in agile teams—then tackled Python-driven machine-learning pipelines at Deventia Tech in Summer 2024, where I cleaned large datasets, trained models with scikit-learn, and visualized insights.

Since September 2024 at Developers 360, I've architected scalable Scrapy and Playwright scraping solutions, automated social-media workflows, and integrated AI models into production. Complemented by side projects—from IoT-enabled smart irrigation to a Django price-comparison platform—and early leadership as Team Manager at Arem Foundation, my versatile toolkit (Python, C++, Django, RPA, Jira/Slack) positions me to deliver high-impact software and data solutions.

Looking ahead, I'm committed to deepening my expertise in data science and artificial intelligence by mastering advanced techniques in machine learning and deep learning, and by contributing to open-source AI frameworks. My plan to architect robust backend systems using Django, emphasizing scalable API design, secure authentication, and seamless integration with data-driven services. By pursuing specialized certifications and collaborating on cross-functional projects, my aim to harness AI-powered analytics to drive smarter decision-making and build next-generation web applications that deliver both performance and meaningful user experiences.
